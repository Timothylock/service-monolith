February 11, 2017 - Laptops

A student asked about bringing their personal laptop to class. I encourage you to do that! That way, you can save your work, and get to use a laptop that you are used to. The only restriction is that you will not be able to connect to our WiFi network for internet.



February 11, 2017 - Roadmap + Borrowing Robots

Good news! After assessing the level of our pros (beginners), I have created a roadmap and anticipate that they will be caught up to the super pros (intermediates) in around 2-3 weeks time. They will begin the advance challenges starting next week. I will then teach the class as a whole.


Some parents have asked about the policy for borrowing our robots. If you feel like you want to bring a robot home to learn, you can begin to sign out the set [base robot + USB cable + charger] starting in March after our ramp-up season is complete. The software used can be downloaded here.
